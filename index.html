Macedonia - Greece Interconnector Intern

As an intern at TSG Industry Service, Macedonia, I was given the responsibility for calculating and minimizing the waste pipe drop-off as a result of welded joints between different sections of the pipeline structure. Using linear algebra, I was able to develop a brute-force algorithm in Python, creating optimal combinations of the provided 12, 14 and 18-meter pipe sections.
